Hi there! I'm Noah.

I'm a software developer, entrepreneur, and spoken poet based in Providence, RI.

We've reached this incredible point in history where technology encompasses most of the world, which means we have the opportunity to revolutionize industry, economy, politics, and the way we treat our planet. By learning from other cultures and using the data poured every day into servers worldwide, we can build sustainable systems to improve everyone's quality of life.
I intend to do exactly that.

I'm a sophomore at Brown University studying Computer Science and Math. I grew up in Frogsong, a Cohousing community in Cotati CA, where 30 families eat, play, and live together as a village. I learned to code at the age of 10, and have been building things ever since.
