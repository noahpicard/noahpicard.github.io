Hi there! I'm Noah.

I'm a software developer, entrepreneur, and spoken poet based in Providence, RI.

Technology encompasses the world, giving us the opportunity to revolutionize industry, economy, politics, and the way we treat our planet. By using new data, applying novel solutions, and learning from each other, we can build sustainable systems to improve everyone's quality of life. I intend to do exactly that.

At age 10, I learned to program games on my friends old Windows ME. In sophomore year of highschool, I started a game design company. Building little characters and worlds, I discovered how tech can help us empathize with the unfamiliar. Later, freelance developing websites and apps, I saw how tech can accent human experience, giving us superpowers. Now, in college, I am putting those ideas into practice, building the tools that I believe will provide aid.

My home is Frogsong, a Cohousing community in Cotati CA, where 30 families eat, play, and live together as a village. I study Computer Science & Math at Brown University.
